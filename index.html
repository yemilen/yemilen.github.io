After that, I worked as a security analyst at XTN Cognitive Security. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Jimma University.

Research Interest

My research focuses on formalising and automating data access under the GDPR and data sharing agreement. I explored policy specification languages such as ODRL and eFLINT to ensures that real-world data sharing agreement align with legal norms and user consent requirements. This involved analysing the expressiveness and limitations of existing languages, bridging social and system level policies and deriving purpose based access control models for GDPR compliant enforcement. In collaboration with healthcare institutions, I have modeled the SIOPE DIPG/DMG data sharing agreement using eFLINT to enabled fined grained access control over clinical data. The approach ensures regulatory compliance throughout data lifecycle by automating manual tasks and minimising human error.
