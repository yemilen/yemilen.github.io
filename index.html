I am an interdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of privacy, legal informatics and access control systems.

I care about designing socio-technical systems that bridge the gap between legal provisions and their technical enforcement, facilitating trust in emerging technologies.

I spend my days between my TA role at the University of Amsterdam and writing my PhD thesis. When I am not doing that, you will find me either cooking, running or watching movies I have seen too many times.

I am originally from Ethiopia and have lived in few places in Europe and now I call the Netherlands home.
